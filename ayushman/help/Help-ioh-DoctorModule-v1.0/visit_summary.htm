Visit Summary

The Visit Summary link in the Past Appointments page displays details of the patient.

 

·Click the Visit Summary link against a patient's name to view the clinical record of the patient's visit/appointment with the doctor.

 

 

 

Visit Summary Details

 

The Visit Summary is a detailed electronic record that the doctor maintains after a patient's visit.

 

·Incidence Visit Number: Lists the visit number
·Date: Displays date of visit
·Name: Displays name of the patient
·Age: Displays age of patient
·Blood Group: Displays blood group of patient
·IOH #: Displays the India Online Health ID of the patient
·Symptom //Main Complaint: List the symptom/nature of complaint of the patient for the visit
·Vital Signs: Lists the vital signs of the patient as typed by the doctor.
·Symptomatic Review of Systems: Displays the doctor's details of the review.
·Examinations: Displays the clinical examination details of patient
·Diagnosis: Displays the diagnosis of the clinical examination during the visit.
·Investigations suggested: Displays the investigations to be carried out by the patient.
·Rx Prescription Displays the doctor's prescription.
·Follow up /  Advice: Displays the follow up plan suggested by the doctor.
·Summary: Details the summary of the clinical examination.
