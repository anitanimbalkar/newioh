Dashboard Consultation

 

The following actions are carried out from the Dashboard Consultations page:

 

·The logged in doctor's Upcoming Appointments are listed.
·Search for a patient by Name or IOH ID.
·View EMR (Electronic Medical Record) of patient.
·Edit EMR of patient.
·Cancel the appointment of a patient.
·Start Consultation of the patient.
·Access electronic EMR, Notes, Trackers, Risk Factors, Prescriptions, Receipts, Clinical Examination Record for recording and saving the patient's clinical details.
·Create and save payment receipt for every visit.
·Create and save only a quick prescription.
·End Consultation at any time without a prescribed time limit.
