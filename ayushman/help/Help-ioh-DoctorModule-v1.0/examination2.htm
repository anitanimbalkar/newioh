Examination

To maintain an electronic record of the detailed examination by your patient, follow the steps below:

 

·Click the Examination button. The various options in the Examination menu displays on the left panel and the selected option displays on the right panel.
·Click the Examination link. The Examination panel on the left displays the options for recording the examination details. The options are:
§Abdomen - Drop-down options available for Inspection, Palpation, Percussion Ascitis and Auscultation
§Back - Drop-down options available are Para-spinal Spasm, Scoliosis, Loss of lumbar lordosis, Lumps, Tenderness, Sensation, Vibration sense, Reflections, Others
§Breast - Drop-down options available are Left-Breast, and Right-Breast
§Cardiac - Drop-down options available are Inspection, Palpation, Percussion Cardiac Outline, Auscultation, Murmur, and Others
§Ears - Drop-down options available are Right-ear and Left-ear
§Extremities - Drop-down options available are Right-lower limb, Left-lower limb, and Others
§Eyes - Drop-down options available are Conjuctival Congestion, Eye Styes, Trachoma, Cornea, Pallor, Icterus, Visual Acuity, Color Vision, Fundus, and Pressure and righht eye and left eye.
§Face - Drop-down options available are Puffiness, Odema, Acne, Rash and Others.
§Feet - Drop-down options available are Left-feet, Right-feet and Others
§Genito-Urinary - Drop-down options available are External genitalia, Rectal, per vaginal examination and others
§Health-Card - Drop-down options available are Height, Weight, Pulse, B.P., Menstrual History, Sexual Problems, Ears, Nose, Eyes, Teeth and Gums, Throat, Skin, Bones and Joint, Per Abdomen, Respiratory, CVS and RNS
§Psychological - Drop-down options available are Mood, Appearance, Anxiety, Restlessness, Irritability, Depression and Others
§Respiratory - Drop-down options available are Right-lung and Left-lung
§Skin - Scalp Hair, Body Hair, Scalp, and Others

.

 

·Click each link under the Examination link. The corresponding page displays.
·Each page displays drop-down options for recording further details.
·Select an option from the drop-down menu or type to record your examination details.
