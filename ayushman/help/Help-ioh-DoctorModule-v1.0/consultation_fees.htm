Consultation Fees

The Set Consultation Fees feature in the MyClinics page allows you to save a pre-scheduled and structured consultation fee for you with the following locations:

 

·Online /  Phone Consultation fee structure - consultation fees for Online/telephonic consultations
·In Clinic Consultation fee structure - consultation fees for a patient visiting your clinic.

 

To structure your consultation fees, follow steps below:

 

 

·First-time fee - Applicable for a patient you are treating the first time in your clinic or online / phone
·Follow-up fee - Applicable for a patient who is returning for the next visit for follow-up / review
·Extension fee - Applicable for a patient whose visit extends the slot duration set by you for treating a patient.

            The extension fee multiplies for every extension of five minutes over and above the slot duration set by you.

 

 

·Only Standard fee option is applicable as of 24th July 2014.
·Concessional and Premium fee options are not available as of 24th July 2014.
