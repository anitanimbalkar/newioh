Schedule Fee Structure

The fee structure is divided into First Time (first time patient has a consultation with the doctor), Follow up (follow up consultation) and Extension (extension of consultation time by every five minutes). Fee is scheduled for Online/Phone consultation and In Clinic consultation.

 

You have two options:

 

·Copy the current fee structure
·Create a scheduled fee structure suitable to your preferences

 

 

Scheduled Fee Structure

 

·To schedule a new fee structure, click on each line below First-time, Follow up, Extension for both Online/Phone and In-clinic consultation. Type the fee structure to suit your preferences.
·Select the date from the drop-down calendar from which you wish to schedule the fee to be applicable in Effective Date.
·Select the check box Allow patient to pay at clinic if you wish to collect fee from the patient in your clinic instead of online.
·Click the Save button to save the new fee structure for copying the fee structure whenever you wish to.
·Click the Reset button if you wish to change any details.
·Click the Scheduled fee structure link on the top of the Set Consultations page to recall and copy the fee structure set and saved by you.
