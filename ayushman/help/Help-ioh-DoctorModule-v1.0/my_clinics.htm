My Clinics

The section My Clinics allows you to create clinics with details such as address, contact details, etc. These clinics will be displayed when the consumer(patients) view your page. For example, when a consumer wishes to fix an appointment with you, they have an option to select Online consultation without visiting your clinic or select any one of your clinics (for a consultation in the selected clinic at the appointed hour).

 

You can add, edit and remove clinics in this section.
