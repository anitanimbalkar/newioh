Add/Remove Diagnostic Center
·The list of Diagnostic Centers listed with India Online Health is displayed on the lower section of the page.
·Click the Add button against a diagnostic center to add the diagnostic center to your page. When a diagnostic center is added, the center is listed and displayed in the My Diagnostic Centers on the upper section of your page.
·Click the Remove button against the name of the diagnostic center to delete the center.
