Appointments

India Online Health allows Doctors to:

 

1.Join IOH on " Practise Administration" basis:
§computerise all medical records, prescriptions
§manage patient appointments electronically

 

 

2.Register for an online plan and have an Online Presence:
§publish profile over web,
§schedule and execute online consultations by video/telephone
§create and store electronic prescriptions
§create, save and maintain electronic payment records and
§create and store electronic alerts and reminders

 

 

3.Register for a Chronic Disease Management Plan:
§create standard and custom-built track sheets for patient records
§have access to structured consultation screens, both systemic and specialty based examination forms
§define a chronic disease management programme, scheduling next check-up and investigation tests and alerts.
