Edit EMR

 

Your Upcoming Appointments dialog box displays on your Dashboard on logging into India Online Health as a doctor.

 

To edit the EMR (Electronic Medical record) of a patient, follow the steps below:

 

·Click the Edit EMR button. An editable version of the History screen of the patient displays.
·The Allergy & Social Habits, Immunization, Personal History, and Family History tabs of the patient are visible.
·Information in these tabs are easily changed/editable.
·Click the Save button to save the changes you have made. The confirmation message displays.
·Later, when you access the EMR page, the saved details are displayed.
