How It Works    
 
IndiaOnlineHealth is a Platform for Consumers, Doctors and other healthcare Service Providers, to come together and bring healthcare to consumer’s doorstep. The platform is suited for follow-up consultation in chronic disease management or minor ailment, where in-person examination is not essential. However, it is NOT designed to handle emergencies or acute cases and consumers are advised to see their doctors or hospitals in such situations.

Consumers:
Our primary objective is for you to connect with your doctor while you are at home or busy at your work place. This requires you to maintain your Medical Folder that can be accessed by you and your Doctor without constraints of time, location or distance.

You need to register yourself as a member of IndiaOnlineHealth Platform and build your medical profile. Your medical records are completely confidential and can be accessed by your Doctor or a person (relative) whom you have decided to give access to. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Consulting your doctor online is a simple 5 step process:

  • Log in as a member. You will see a member’s dashboard.
  • Search and Create your "Panel of Doctors" from list of registered doctors.
  • Fix an appointment convenient to you and your doctor.
  • Connect and Consult (via Video and / or Audio) at appointed time.
  • Your Doctor updates the EMR and provides a consultation summary (including diagnosis, prescription, investigations request, follow up advice).
  

Scheduling a Diagnostic Test is convenient:
While creating your On-line visit summary, your Doctor may have asked for Diagnostic tests and other investigations. Now you can connect with Diagnostic Labs for an appointment or ask them for a home visit to collect samples, wherever, practical.

  • Select a lab from your Network of diagnostic centers.
  • Provide the Investigation Requisition Form. Your lab’s catalog will open and you will be able to see the charges. You can place the order and fix an appointment.
  • Once the reports are ready, they will be uploaded into your EMR.

Ordering of Medicines:
At this time, you cannot order the medicine on-line. However, you can forward the e-Prescription to your Chemist. He can keep your supplies ready and you can pick at your convenience. He can also let you know if some medicines are out of stock. This will save you precious time. Since the Prescription is in Electronic format, there is no issue of illegible handwriting.

Doctor:
If you are a practicing Doctor, you may register on the IndiaOnlineHealth by filling out a Registration Form. Our back end team will do the validation. Once registered, you declare your on-line consultation calendar and your consultation charges. That is it. You are all set to provide On-line consultation to your patients anytime convenient to you and from anywhere.

Once you log-in, you will see your dashboard outlining your appointments as well as on-line status of your patients.

    

You will be able to view EMR of your patient before starting the consultation. At appointed time, you can start consultation session with your patient. You can record your observations, diagnosis, consultation summary, as well as write an e-Prescription and request for investigations.
