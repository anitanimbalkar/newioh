Consumers    
 

Individual consumers:
There are several ways in which use of India Online Health platform can benefit the individual consumers.

  • Centralize storage of Electronic Medical Record (EMR)
  • Video / Audio Consultation with their Doctors
  • Second Opinion from Specialist
  • Remote Participation in consultation of close relatives
  • Scheduling of Pathology tests at your lab
  • e-Prescription to your chemist for ready pick up

Special Interest Groups:
Cooperative Housing Societies, large residential complex and Senior Citizen Homes can create Virtual Clinics equipped with basic tele-consulting infrastructure supported by a paramedic to enable their residents to use India Online Health Platform. All that is needed is a Computer, Broadband, Webcam and an admin support for running the Virtual Clinic. This can provide tremendous benefit to senior citizens and those not very comfortable with computers to avail near-presence consulting experience without the hassles of commuting to the clinic and long wait. It will also provide great relief to their children at work.

Corporate Users:
Several corporate companies offer benefits and programs to their employees for managing health and wellness. Many organizations have in-campus doctors too. However, employees have confidence in their own panel of doctors.
India Online HealthPlatform provides ideal opportunity for corporate organizations to extend and enhance these benefits to employees and their families.

  • Employees can consult their family physicians or Specialists from their workplace.
  • Employees can participate in consultation of their family members from their workplace resulting in greater level of satisfaction and comfort.
  
  • Employees can seek ‘second opinion’ from specialists from their work place or home with their EMR in the backdrop.
  • Family members can consult in-campus doctors from home or other remote location.
  • Traveling employees can stay in touch with their family doctors from out-station.
  • Factories located away from city center can provide ‘specialists’ access to their work force and factory located GPs too can seek advice from specialists
