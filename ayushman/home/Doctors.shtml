Doctors    
 

As a doctor, your time is precious. During peak Clinic hours, you may want to see new patients and move follow-up consultations to off-peak slots and from the comfort of your home. Through use of our platform, you can encourage your patients to use on-line consultation hours. You still have complete access to their medical records, and provide consultation over Video / phone, without compromising on your consultation charges. You can extend your reach and provide consulting to remote locations – outskirts of major towns, rural areas or other cities/geographies. Work-life balance is important but doctors find it difficult to take vacations due to lack of alternative care for their patients. You can stay connected with your critical patients while on vacation.

Many times, you may feel the need to collaborate with another doctor. Our platform enables you to connect with a Specialist along with your patient in the same call. Both doctors have access to the same medical records.

  

Our platform also enables you to provide continuity of consulting to your patients even if they are traveling or have relocated to a new location or city.

Membership Charges:
Your Doctor membership is completely complimentary. As a Doctor, you decide your online consultation hours and your consultation charges.
